Green Smoothie

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 handfuls baby spinach
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks frozen
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients into the blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add more almond milk or ice ifthe smoothie is too thick.
  3. Sprinkle chia seeds and serve.
