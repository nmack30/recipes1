French Toast with Blueberries and Banana

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 thick slices of bread
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/2 tsp almond extract, optional
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of blueberries
  • maple syrup

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, salt, and optional almond extract until throughly blended.
  2. Preheat skillet on medium heat and melt about a tablespoon of butter.
  3. Dip one slice of bread into the egg mixture. Turn to coat other side of bread. Allow any excess liquid to drain.
  4. Cook bread until browned on one side. It should take about two minutes. Before flipping, add two pinches of sugar. Flip and brown the other side for two minutes.
  5. Repeat with the remaining bread.
  6. Immediately serve your warm, delicious French toast with slices of bananas, fresh blueberries, and maple syrup.
