Avocado Egg Toast

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner

One of my favorite breakfast meal is avocado toast. It tastes even better with a medium-fried egg. It's so easy and quick to prepare. Some of my favorite toppings to add to this simple dish are lime juice, onion, tomato, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 4 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Mash the avacado. Add lime juice, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Fry the eggs in a pan over medium heat. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Toast your bread.
  4. Spread avocado mixture on the toast. Top with fried egg.

Optional

  • Add basil or any herbs to the avocado dish.
  • Add bacon.
  • Add chili pepper.
