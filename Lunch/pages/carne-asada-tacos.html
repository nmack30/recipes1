Carne Asada Tacos

Home Breakfast Lunch dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 lbs flank steak
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 fresh limes
  • 1 bundle of cilantro
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 16 mini corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Thinly slice the steak. Then chop into bite size pieces.
  2. In a skillet, begin to brown the steak in the oil.
  3. Add half the onions to the skillet and cook until soft.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook until the meat is cooked through.
  6. While the steak is cooking, melt the butter in another skillet.
  7. Fry the mini corn tortillas in the melted butter for 1 minute until they're soft.
  8. Allow them to cool. You will need two mini corn tortillas per tacos.
  9. Spoon the meat mixture on the tortillas.
  10. Top with cilantro, fresh onions, and serve with limes.
