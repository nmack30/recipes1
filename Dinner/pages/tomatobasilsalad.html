Tomato Basil Salad

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 6 medium-size slices mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. For salad, arrange tomato slices and maozzarella cheese on a large plate or platter. Sprinkle with basil.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together dressing ingredients. Pour over tomatoes and mozarella cheese.
